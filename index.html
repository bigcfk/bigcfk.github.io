Kieran Conor

  • Kieran Conor speaks rhythmically over hip-hop beats and doesn't really like talking about himself in the third-person. Instead of rapping simply about "sex, drugs, and money," he talks about his lack thereof, and tells stories about everyday experiences intertwined with clever wordplay and tongue-in-cheek lyrics.

    People have described Kieran Conor as "a nice guy rapper," and have said his music is like "a guided mediation" or "how Jesus would've rapped" (only because of the storytelling; water remains water when Kieran speaks). He'll put words in your ears that are guaranteed to put a smile on your face.

  • Hey! I'm Kieran Conor! Welcome to my world wide webpage. I am a writer, teacher, videographer, and dude. I have a couple other metaphoric hats, too. I write songs and stories (other things, too, but that sounded nice. I'm also a poet.)

    I perform the songs I write at open mics occasionally. I've recently decided to record my music, partly to share it with people, and partly for posterity's sake.

    The stories I write are what ultimately led me to film production, sometimes referred to as 'videography.' I film narrative scenes, concerts, and music videos. A buddy and I started a multimedia group, Active Leisure Media.

    An online presence is arguably a necessity these days because cyberspace has become the primary medium for just about everything. This website's main objective is to earnestly present what I can do and who I am to the awe-inspiring abyss of anonymity, known as the Internet.
